How to Play

  • Click a cell to reveal it.
  • Numbers show what's hiding in adjacent cells:
    • Red number = bombs nearby
    • Blue number = letters nearby
  • Avoid 💣 bombs — hit 3 and it's game over.
  • Letters you reveal are collected in the pool below the board. Find all 5, then unscramble them to guess the word.
  • You can guess any time — you don't have to find all the letters first.
  • Right-click (desktop) or long-press (mobile) to 🚩 flag a cell you think is a bomb.

Hard Mode

  • 10×10 grid with more bombs and 3 decoy letters mixed in among the real ones.
  • The blue numbers only count real word letters — decoys are invisible to the numbers. Use the clues to figure out which letters actually belong to the word.
  • Letters don't go to the pool automatically. Click a revealed letter to add it; click again to remove it.
  • Enable Hard Mode via the ⚙ Settings button. Your preference carries over to the next day's puzzle.
