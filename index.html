How to Play

  • Click a cell to reveal it.
  • Numbers show what's hiding in adjacent cells:
    • Red number = bombs nearby
    • Blue number = letters nearby
  • Avoid 💣 bombs — hit 3 and it's game over.
  • Letters you reveal are collected in the pool below the board. Find all 5, then unscramble them to guess the word.
  • Feeling lucky? Hit "Guess Early" to guess before finding all the letters.
  • Right-click (desktop) or long-press (mobile) to 🚩 flag a cell you think is a bomb.
